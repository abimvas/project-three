Additional Temperature Visualizations

Javier Lopez, Matt Shuff, Jordan Tata, Abigail Vasquez, Jasmine (Lo) Winata.

This bubble chart made in tableau shows the temperature trends in China, Japan, and the US. the maximum values occured in 2013 for China and US which is the most recent data included in out dataset. Japan's max temperature occured in 1990.

This line scatter made with seaborn shows temperature trends above and below 50 degrees latitutude closer to the arctic. A decrese in temps occured from 1800-1850, followed by a slow increase that picks up velocity around 1980 onward.
