Predicting Future Temperatures using Machine Learning

Javier Lopez, Matt Shuff, Jordan Tata, Abigail Vasquez, Jasmine (Lo) Winata.

Machine Learning

For this dataset we used a Linear Regression model to predict future tempature. We originally plotted the year against the average temperature in degrees farenheit and found that there was a positive relationship as the year increases, the average temperature also increases.

Jupyter Notebook: linear_regression.ipynb

After creating a Linear Regression model we were able to see a small increase in temperature over time and confirmed that over time the global average temperature increases.
